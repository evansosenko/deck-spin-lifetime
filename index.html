Device geometry

$$D ∇^2 μ_s - \frac{μ_s}{τ} + ω × μ_s = 0$$

$$V ∝ μ_s^N(x = L)$$

  • \( L \) : contact spacing
  • \( D \) : diffusion constant
  • \( τ \) : spin lifetime
  • \( ω = g μ_B B / ħ \)
  • \( μ_s = \frac{1}{2} \left( μ_↑ - μ_↓ \right) \)
  • \( J_{↑↓} = σ_{↑↓} ∇μ_{↑↓} \)
  • \( J_{↑↓}^C = Σ_{↑↓} \left( μ^N_{↑↓} - μ^F_{↑↓} \right)_c \)
  • \( J = J_↑ + J_↓ \)
  • \( J_s = J_↑ - J_↓ \)
